COIN-OPERATED CAR WASH SYSTEM

The use of COIN-OPERATED CAR WASH SYSTEM has become a common and useful way for car owners to maintain the cleanliness and aesthetic appeal of their vehicles without having to spend the time-consuming effort of manual washing.



About

ABSTRACT

This project included parts such as the coin sensor, vending machine, GSM module, and power wash system, utilizing the Arduino Uno's full capability as an open-source electronics platform. It enabled the GSM module to receive sensor data, send SMS messages to the owner's phone number, and identify coin values entered the vending machine. The power wash feature was designed to run according to the number of minutes per 5-peso coin to ensure the effective use of resources. In addition, the Blynk application provided owners with the ability to get real-time income updates via SMS notifications, further enhancing the operation's effectiveness and transparency.



HARDWARE SPECIFICATION

In the development of Coin Operated Car Wash with SMS Notification, the following were the required hardware specification:



ARDUINO UNO

A popular and commonly used microcontroller development board for the Arduino platform is the Arduino Uno.



VENDING MACHINE

It is the machine to be used for coin insertion to have a self-service car wash.



POWER WASH MACHINE

Power washing machine or pressure washing machine is the cleaning tool that involves applying a high-pressure water spray to various surfaces to get rid of dust, filth, mildew, paint that has come loose, dirt, and other impurities of an automobile.



WATER HOSE

It is the flexible tube or pipe to be used to transport water from one place to another.



MOBILE DEVICE

It is the device for receiving the SMS notification of the income of the coin-operated car wash.



SIM CARD

The sim card phone number used as a contact number of which the notification was sent.



COIN SLOT

It was the one to detect the value of the coin being inserted in the vending machine.
